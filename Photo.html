Interest Photography

I've always enjoyed taking photos because I love the concept of capturing a moment in time forever. I love looking at a photo and being able to connect with the moment in time. Sometimes I can relive a whole moment just by glancing at a photo. I started taking photos when I was 8 years old. My dad got me this cute pink poloroid camera. It wasnt a poloroid brand but it was like a kids version that would print film photos as soon as you took them. I remember scrapbooking a bunch of photos of my cat and my dad always reminding me to, "branch out because theres more to life than pebbles(my cat at the time)."
