Family My Mother

My mother is my favorite person in the world. She is the strongest woman I’ve ever known. She’s insanely outspoken and hilarious. (and I’m not just saying that because she made me lol). I’ve never met an individual who genuinely does not care what anyone thinks about her. She’s extremely kind but has no problem telling ANYONE when they're wrong. She established great morals in me as a child and always made sure I stayed honest. She’s lived a hard life and I’ve watched her overcome so many battles. I’m not sure how she survived raising me because honestly I wasn’t the best child ever but she was always there for me when I needed her. Even if I was being stubborn and didn’t want her help she still managed to make her way in and lift me up. My mother has truly been the only person I could run to for my entire life and I will always be grateful for her and the love she gives me.
