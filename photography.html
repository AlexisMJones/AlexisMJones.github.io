Photography My Photos

Tower

I took this photo in Tower district a couple years ago. This is still one of my favorite photos because when I first moved to Fresno, Tower was my favorite place to go. I'd go down there for shows, to have lunch, and during the week I'd study at my favorite coffee shop. Everytime I look at this photo it makes me smile and reminds me why I love Fresno so much.

Palm Tree

I took this photo while I was hanging out on Fresno City Campus. This was taken my first semester at Fresno City. I use to just spend my breaks laying out on the grass taking photos when I got the chance. Now that Covid has happened I miss times like these. Whenever I look at this photo it reminds me to cherish the little things.

Music
